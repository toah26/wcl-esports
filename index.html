Who We Are

Built for the long season.

World Class was built to do exactly what the name says. We field competitive rosters across Rocket League, Fortnite, and Valorant, backing players who want to compete at the highest level and represent something bigger than a single tournament run.

We're still early — building rosters, locking in staff, and growing division by division. What doesn't change is the standard: talented players, real structure, and a brand worth competing under.
